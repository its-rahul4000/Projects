## Main Hardware Casing 
This is the main hardware casing in form of 3d printed box - casing for our device that includes battery and main PCB. 

### Dimensions of Box - 
Length: 48 mm
Width: 40 mm
Height: 19 mm

### Challenge faced - 

1. Incorrect Initial Cable Hole Placement
* A hole was initially designed on the side of the enclosure to route the shielded cable connecting the main box to the metallic sensor casing. The first hole location did not provide sufficient tolerance for proper cable routing, making the connection difficult.
* Due to misalignment and space constraints, the cable could not pass smoothly through the intended path.
*As a result, an additional hole was drilled on the opposite side of the box after 3D printing was done.

2. Tolerance Issue Between Lid and Box
* During the initial design, the tolerance between the lid and the box was slightly larger than required.
* Because of this, the lid did not hold the box securely, resulting in a loose fit.
* The issue required reconsideration of the snap-fit design and adjustment of tolerances.
Design modifications were made to achieve a tighter and more reliable fit between the lid and the box.
