## Lid Hardware Casing

This is the main hardware casing in form of 3d printed box - casing for our device that includes battery and main PCB.

### Dimensions of Box -

Length: 48 mm Width: 40 mm Height: 6 mm
With 2 mm round edges, 1 mm top thickness, and 2.5 mm edge thickness.

### Challenge faced -

A hole was initially designed on the side of the enclosure to route the shielded cable connecting the main box to the metallic sensor casing. However, the first hole location did not provide sufficient tolerance for proper cable routing, making the connection difficult. As a result, an additional hole was created on the opposite side of the box. In the revised design, the hole was intentionally positioned such that it was split between the main box and the lid edge, allowing the cable to pass through the interface between them when assembled. This ensured better alignment and a smoother cable passage while maintaining enclosure usability.
